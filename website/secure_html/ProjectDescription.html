Problem Statement

Pest encounters in communities are a common occurrence, but due to the isolated nature of these experiences and fragmented means of reporting, community awareness and an informed collective response rarely occurs.



Problem Characteristics

  • Members in a community have no reliable way to stay informed on local pest encounters


  • Many preventable pest encounters still happen because there is no real-time awareness of their presence and location


  • People with pest infestation problems cannot easily benefit from previous learning experiences and/or guidance of their community because it is either not available or not consolidated


  • When planning outdoor activities or trips to other locations, it is difficult to effectively prepare for active pest problems because the information does not exist



Solution Statement

Pest Patrol is an application that will allow individuals to report, monitor and communicate the overall pest activity in their community; as well as allow users to coordinate a community based solution to pest problems.



Solution Characteristics

  • Allow members in a community to stay informed on local pest encounters by setting their location and receiving alerts when incidents appear in their area


  • Help prevent pest encounters by providing awareness of the presence and location of undesirable invaders through user reports


  • Enable communities to track outbreaks by enabling heat mapping based on user-defined parameters


  • Assist with aggregating knowledge of previous effective pest control strategies within a community


  • Grant community members the ability to safely plan outdoor activities and trips by providing up-to-date information regarding pest problems at specific locations
